# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Preloop community a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but listen when impact is called out.
- Focus feedback on ideas, code, and behavior, not people.
- Help keep discussions welcoming to contributors of all experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact [conduct@preloop.ai](mailto:conduct@preloop.ai).

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
